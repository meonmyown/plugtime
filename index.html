The Begining

When a man is born it is quite a period of celebration for the parents, family, friends and even the neighbors are not even excluded in this jubilation. The cry of the child brings nothing but happiness to all those who hears it; for the moment a crying child is being celebrated instead of being consoled or given food or what the child wants in other to stop crying.

The Life

As he grows older he is considered the child of the community, as he learns from everyone around; be it by words of advice or by the experience of the things he sees around or sometimes even from his very own experience. He learns on each passing day and continues to grow daily, he makes friends, co-workers, distance family members that he can call loved ones and those to call upon when need.

The End

At this particular time in the man's live, he is quiet, not crying and also motionless; but the silence brings about tears and crying from both the friends, family, neighbors even co-workers and extended family members. He was loved by all people then begins to sing his praises. But at this particular he has crossed the veil already to the other side to be reunited with his dead acenstors.
